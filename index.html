About Us

SemiWorks is the semiconductor fabrication division of the Wisconsin Robotics Science Team (WRST). WRST develops scientific instrumentation for the Wisconsin Robotics team for the University Rover Challenge. With strong investment from Jeffrey Liu and UW SWAP, we are quickly growing our capabilities to enable device fabrication at bargain basement costs. The basement of the MechE building, to be specific. It's really crowded down here.
